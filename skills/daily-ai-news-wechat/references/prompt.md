# Summary Prompt Template

---

You are given an article about AI, large model application development, agent development, or skill development. Please summarize the key points:

1. **Main topic/核心主题**: What is the main subject of this article?
2. **Key insights/主要观点**: What are the most important takeaways?
3. **Practical information/实用信息**: Are there any specific tools, techniques, or resources mentioned that developers would find useful?

Keep your summary concise (100-200 words) and focus on information that is valuable to AI developers.
